TableRBOTone

TableRBOTone[ghtt_,ghbb_,x_,xmin_,xmax_,xstep_]
This command generates a table of the signal strength

. The arguments ghtt are ghbb are the htt and hbb couplings. Here, h represents to SM-like Higgs boson while t and b are the top and bottom quarks. The label x indicates the parameter to constraint, while xmin and xmax are the initial and final values defined by users and xstep is used to indicates the steps from xmin to xmax.
