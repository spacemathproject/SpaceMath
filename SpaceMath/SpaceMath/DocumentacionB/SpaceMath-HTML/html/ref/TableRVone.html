TableRVone

TableRVone[ghtt_,ghbb_,ghVV_,x_,xmin_,xmax_,xstep_]
This command generates a table of the signal strength

, with V= W, Z. The arguments ghtt, ghbb and ghVV are the htt, hbb and hVV couplings. Here, h represents to SM-like Higgs boson while t and b the top and bottom quarks. The label x indicates the parameter to constraint, while xmin and xmax are the initial and final values defined by users and xstep is used to indicates the steps from xmin to xmax.
